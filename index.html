Info

Currently I am a post-doc researcher at the Department for Information and Communication Systems, Faculty of electrical engineering, mechanical engineering, and naval architecture (FESB), University of Split, Croatia.

I got my Ph.D. in Computer Science as a part of a research project "ICT systems and services based on information integration" (023-0231924-1661). My adviser was prof. Nikola Rožić. My thesis was on improving the Quality of RFID systems (pdf - to appear). I got my M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Split. (Download CV)

Research Overview

The main focus of my research is improving the Quality of Service within Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) systems, where I have dealt with a number of problems surrounding design and evaluation of PHY, MAC and APP layer protocols.
